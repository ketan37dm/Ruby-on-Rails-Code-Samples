FAQ's

What payment types do you accept?

Currently, we accept PayPal, Google Checkout, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. If you would like to pay with a check or money order, just send us an email and we will be in touch as soon as possible.

How do I add or remove users?

Adding or removing users from your account takes only seconds. Simply go to the Personnel page within your account, where you have the option to add or delete additional coaches, players, or auxiliary users.

Can we add more sports to the High School Edge later?

You bet. If more teams at your program would like to sign up, the Account Manager can easily create additional sports. Account Managers also have the ability to create JV, Freshman or any other sub level of competition for each sport.

What levels of competition do you support?

We can work with any sport at any level of competition. Teams that want to have easy access to their important content and improve communication between the coaches and players, MyTeamsEdge is for them.
